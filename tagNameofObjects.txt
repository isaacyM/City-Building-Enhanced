AOE3 DE Mod – Extended Age Progression System

Goal:

* Reduce age rushing.
* Make economic development matter.
* Extend gameplay in Colonial (II) and Fortress (III).
* Make Industrial (IV) a significant milestone.
* Make Imperial (V) a true late-game achievement.
* Encourage balanced economies instead of pure food gathering.

Age II – Colonial Age
Cost:

* 1000 Food
* 200 Wood

Research Time:

* 120 seconds

Purpose:

* Slightly delays early rushes.
* Requires players to invest in economy before aging.
* Keeps opening strategies mostly intact.

Age III – Fortress Age
Cost:

* 2000 Food
* 500 Wood
* 500 Coin

Research Time:

* 180 seconds

Purpose:

* First major economic commitment.
* Encourages development of all resource types.
* Extends Colonial warfare and map control.

Age IV – Industrial Age
Cost:

* 4000 Food
* 1500 Wood
* 1500 Coin

Research Time:

* 240 seconds

Purpose:

* Industrial becomes a strategic decision rather than an automatic progression.
* Strong reward for economic planning.
* Creates longer Fortress Age battles.

Age V – Imperial Age
Cost:

* 8000 Food
* 3000 Wood
* 3000 Coin

Research Time:

* 300 seconds

Purpose:

* True endgame technology tier.
* Reaching Imperial should feel earned.
* Encourages long-term resource planning.

Expected Match Flow

Age I:

* 5–8 minutes
* Scouting and economic setup.

Age II:

* 15–25 minutes
* Main combat phase.
* Most focus-tree decisions occur here.

Age III:

* 20–40 minutes
* Large-scale battles.
* Advanced military compositions become available.

Age IV:

* Late-game power spike.
* High-tier upgrades and units become available.

Age V:

* Rare endgame age.
* Reserved for long matches and economic dominance.

Design Philosophy

The objective is not to prevent aging but to make each age a meaningful economic investment.

Players should:

* Need Food, Wood, and Coin to progress.
* Spend more time utilizing units and upgrades available in Ages II and III.
* View Industrial and Imperial as strategic milestones instead of automatic progression paths.

This progression is intended to complement:

* Military Focus Trees
* Command Post system
* Conscription upgrades
* Expanded unit rosters
* Longer, more strategic matches
